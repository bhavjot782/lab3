The Importance Of Forests

Deforestation, fueled by human actions like farming and logging, threatens forests globally. These ecosystems are essential for regulating climate, filtering air and water, and providing habitats for diverse wildlife. Protecting forests is essential for keeping biodiversity and fighting climate change
